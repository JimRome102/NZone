SENDING TIPS & BEST PRACTICES
================================================================================

1. TIMING
   - Send emails Tuesday-Thursday mornings (9-11am local time preferred)
   - Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (lower priority)
   - Directors are often in meetings; give them 3-5 business days to respond

2. PERSONALIZATION
   - Use director's name when available
   - Reference the specific center location
   - Mention the city name to show genuine local knowledge
   - Keep tone warm, professional, not salesy

3. FOLLOW-UP STRATEGY
   - Template 1: Initial outreach
   - Wait 5-7 days
   - Template 2: Polite follow-up
   - Wait another 5-7 days
   - Template 3: Alternative angle OR move to next prospect
   - If still no response, consider a phone call if number is available

4. CONTACT METHOD PRIORITY
   - Email first (less intrusive, allows director to respond on their schedule)
   - Phone follow-up after email (if number available from business listing)
   - LinkedIn message as alternative (if director has visible profile)
   - Center general line as last resort (ask for director's email)

5. PHONE CALL SCRIPT (if you reach director directly)
   "Hi [Director Name], this is [Your Name] with Nzone Sports. I sent you an email
   earlier this week about a potential partnership opportunity for your center. Do you
   have 10 minutes to chat about it? ...I specialize in helping Kindercare centers add
   youth sports programs for families."

6. RESPONSE RATE EXPECTATIONS
   - Initial response rate: 5-10% typical for cold outreach
   - If director expresses interest, next step is a call to discuss specifics
   - Always ask: "What does a good solution look like for your center?"

7. TRACKING
   - Keep a simple spreadsheet noting: date sent, template used, response
   - Update status as you receive replies or follow up
   - Note any specific feedback or objections for future improvement
