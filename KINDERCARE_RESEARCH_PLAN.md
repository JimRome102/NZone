# Nzone x Kindercare Partnership Research Plan

## Objective
Find decision makers at individual Kindercare locations with phone numbers and email addresses. Target Center Directors/Managers who can authorize youth sports programming partnerships. Build contact list for grassroots outreach campaign to sell Nzone partnership.

## Phase 1: Identify Kindercare Locations & Decision Makers

- [ ] Find Kindercare locations (use Google Maps, Yellow Pages, Kindercare website locator)
- [ ] Identify geographic focus area (state, region, or nationwide)
- [ ] Determine franchise vs. corporate-owned locations
- [ ] Research typical Kindercare location organizational structure:
  - Center Director (primary decision maker)
  - Assistant Director
  - Area/Regional Supervisor (oversees multiple centers)
  - Franchise Owner (if applicable)

## Phase 2: Find Target Individuals at Each Location

Target roles (in order of outreach priority):
1. Center Director (primary decision maker for programs)
2. Area/Regional Supervisor (can push initiative across multiple locations)
3. Franchise Owner (if applicable to that location)

Research methods:
- [ ] Call Kindercare locations directly for Director name/contact
- [ ] Search Google Maps/online directories for location phone numbers
- [ ] Check Kindercare location websites (many list staff)
- [ ] Search LinkedIn for Kindercare Directors (may show location)
- [ ] Use business databases (ZoomInfo, Apollo.io, Hunter.io for emails)
- [ ] Check Facebook pages for Kindercare locations (often list staff)

## Phase 3: Collect Contact Information

For each location:
- [ ] Location name and address
- [ ] Phone number
- [ ] Director/Manager name
- [ ] Director email (if available)
- [ ] Director phone (if available)
- [ ] Area Supervisor name/contact (if applicable)
- [ ] LinkedIn profile link
- [ ] Notes on best contact method

Data collection tools:
- [ ] Direct phone calls to locations
- [ ] Business listing aggregators (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Hunter)
- [ ] LinkedIn direct search
- [ ] Google Maps business info
- [ ] Email pattern generation if needed

## Phase 4: Create Outreach Strategy

### Spreadsheet Structure
- Location Name
- Location Address/City
- Phone Number
- Director Name
- Director Email
- Director Phone
- Area Supervisor (if known)
- Best Contact Method
- LinkedIn URL
- Contact Status

### Outreach Templates
Create 2-3 templates:
1. **Cold Call Script** - For initial phone contact with Director
2. **Email Pitch** - Short email highlighting parent benefits and easy implementation
3. **Follow-up** - If no response, reference previous outreach

### Outreach Sequence
1. **Phone Call** - Call Director directly to introduce Nzone and gauge interest
2. **Email Follow-up** - Send pitch email with info if interested
3. **Second Contact** - Try different contact method (Area Supervisor, call back) if no response
4. **Demo/Meeting** - Schedule call/meeting if Director interested

## Deliverables
- [ ] Excel/CSV spreadsheet with Kindercare location contacts (organized by region/state)
- [ ] Cold call script
- [ ] Email pitch template (customizable)
- [ ] Follow-up email template
- [ ] Tracking sheet for outreach status and responses

## Strategy Notes
- **Target:** Start with high-volume areas or specific regions to test
- **Success Metric:** Director interest in piloting program at their location
- **Selling Points for Directors:**
  - Adds value for parents (after-school/youth programming)
  - Easy implementation (you handle recruitment/management)
  - Revenue share potential for location
  - Differentiates their center from competitors
