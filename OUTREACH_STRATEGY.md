# Nzone Kindercare Partnership Outreach Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the strategy for reaching out to Atlanta metro Kindercare directors about the Nzone youth sports partnership.

---

## Phase 1: Preparation (Week 1)

- [ ] Customize email templates with your information
- [ ] Organize contact list by geography (clusters for easier meetings)
- [ ] Create tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Set up email/phone system for responses
- [ ] Prepare Nzone pitch deck or one-pager

---

## Phase 2: Initial Outreach (Week 2-3)

**Batch 1: 15-20 locations**

1. Send **Email Template 1** on Tuesday-Thursday morning
2. Wait 5-7 days for responses
3. Follow up non-responders with **Email Template 2**
4. Wait another 5-7 days
5. Final reach-out: **Phone call** or **Email Template 3**

**Tracking:**
- Date sent
- Response received (yes/no)
- Response quality
- Next action needed

---

## Phase 3: Second Batch (Week 4-5)

Based on results from Batch 1:
- If response rate is good (5%+): Scale to Batch 2 (20-30 locations)
- If response rate is low: Refine messaging and try new angle
- Identify patterns (geography, size, type of location)

---

## Phase 4: Conversions (Week 6+)

**For interested directors:**
1. Schedule 15-min call to explain partnership
2. Send contract/partnership agreement
3. Discuss logistics and timeline
4. Launch pilot program

**For warm leads:**
- Keep in CRM
- Follow up monthly
- Share success stories
- Build relationship over time

## Common Objections & Responses

### "We already have other programs"
**Response:** "Totally understand. Nzone is different because parents sign up directly and you don't manage it. It's an add-on, not a burden."

### "Our families don't want after-school sports"
**Response:** "That might be true - but some will. Even if 10-15 families sign up, that's revenue and added value. No risk since we handle it all."

### "How much does it cost?"
**Response:** "There's no cost to you. You provide facility space, and we handle recruitment, coaching, and management. You could also earn revenue share if interested."

### "Let me think about it"
**Response:** "Of course! When would be a good time to follow up? [Note date - follow up in 2-3 weeks]"

### "I'm not the decision maker"
**Response:** "Who would be the right person to talk to about new programming?" [Get contact info and reach out]
